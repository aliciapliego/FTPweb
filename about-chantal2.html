Prof. Dr-Med Chantal Pauli

Principal Investigator

Prof. Chantal Pauli is an assistant professor with tenure track for systems pathology and functional tumor pathology at the University of Zurich and University Hospital in Zurich, Switzerland. She is a board certified pathologist and is leading the soft tissue and bone pathology service in the Department for Pathology and Molecular Pathology at the University Hospital in Zurich, Switzerland.

With her interest in functional precision oncology she aims to improve individual patient care by using medium to high throughput drug profiling of patient derived ex-vivo cancer models. She is leading the laboratory for Systems Pathology and Functional Tumor Pathology (FTP lab) since July 2020. Her research interests are mainly in rare disease such as soft tissue sarcoma and cancer of unknown primary, ex-vivo modeling and functional screens for drug target discoveries and the identification of novel treatment strategies.
